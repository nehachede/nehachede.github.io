During my Master's in Data Science at Indiana University, I had the opportunity to work with [Prof. Girish Mallapragada](https://kelley.iu.edu/faculty-research/faculty-directory/profile.html?id=gmallapr) at the Kelley School of Business on projects focused on building scalable data pipelines, automating validation workflows, and implementing reproducible research infrastructure.

### Key Contributions

- Built scalable data collection pipelines to extract 1,500+ articles across 800+ firms using Factiva and its Query Genius tool for targeted searches. <i>Factiva provides full-text access to national and international newspapers, trade publications, business newswires, media transcripts, news photos, investment analyst reports, market research, and company profiles, enabling optimized workflows for research on new product introductions and corporate programs.</i>

- Applied Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques, including Named Entity Recognition (NER), Regex and custom entity extraction, to process 300+ unstructured movie summaries, improving product placement analysis efficiency by 40% and achieving ~3x faster throughput.

- Supported IPO data analysis, tracking 1000+ IPO announcements and extracting actionable insights for research on new product introductions.

- Automated data validation and transformation workflows using Python and SQL, creating rule-based checks to identify duplicates, missing fields, and inconsistencies, reducing recurring data quality issues by 30% and saving ~10 hours per week in manual verification.

- Established version-controlled workflows using Git and structured documentation to support reproducible, collaborative research and long-term project scalability.

<i>Skills: Python, SQL, NLP, NER, Regex, Data Quality, Version Control, Workflow Optimization</i>
