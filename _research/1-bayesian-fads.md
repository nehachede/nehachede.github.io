During **Summer 2024**, I had the opportunity to collaborate with **Professor Jeremy Price, School of Education, Indiana University**, as part of the **Faculty Assistance in Data Science (FADS) – IU Research program**. I worked on **quantitatively measuring the impact of community-engaged research** using network modeling, Bayesian alignment metrics, and interactive visualizations.  

To explore the full details and technical insights of this project, please visit my **Research Projects** page: [Research](/research).
