Hi, my name is **Neha Chede**, a Data Scientist and Decision Analytics professional passionate about turning complex data into actionable insights that drive measurable impact.

I hold a Master’s in Data Science from Indiana University and a Bachelor’s in Information Technology from Savitribai Phule Pune University.

I started my career at **Mu Sigma**, delivering statistical analyses, machine learning–driven analytics, and strategic insights for global portfolios, and eventually led these portfolios, managing delivery, client engagement, and strategic priorities. Later, at **Indiana University**, I contributed to research analytics, focusing on reproducible workflows, data quality validation, and NLP-based projects.

Currently, I collaborate with **Heartland Community Network**, designing data frameworks and interactive dashboards to support evidence-based decisions for underserved communities.

Beyond my professional work, I am actively involved in **Society of Women Engineers** and **Women in Tech**, supporting mentorship, representation, and inclusive growth in technical fields.

I am currently seeking full-time opportunities where I can leverage my analytical, technical, and problem-solving skills. I believe data science is most powerful when it combines technology, math, and business to translate complex data into actionable, evidence-driven strategy. Let’s connect and explore how data can drive meaningful impact together!
