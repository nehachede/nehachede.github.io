I focused on integrating programming and image analysis to improve efficiency, accuracy, and reproducibility in experimental documentation.

Key Contributions: 

- Developed a cross-platform Java/JavaFX desktop application to automate HPTLC plate imaging and data collection.

- Leveraged OpenCV and advanced image processing techniques for qualitative and quantitative analysis of chromatograms.

- Streamlined lab evaluation workflows, reducing manual analysis time by 68% and improving accuracy of experimental documentation.

- Implemented an intuitive GUI and automated reporting, enabling multiple experiments to be processed efficiently and consistently.

<b> This internship provided my first exposure to applying software solutions to real-world scientific problems, giving me a tangible sense of how automation can accelerate research outcomes and create measurable impact.</b>
