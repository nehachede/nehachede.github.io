While pursuing my Master's in Data Science at Indiana University, I worked as a Data Quality Analyst at the [Kelley School of Business](https://kelley.iu.edu/index.html) and as a Research Assistant at the [Pervasive Technology Institute (PTI)](https://pti.iu.edu/). I focused on building scalable data collection systems, automating validation workflows, and implementing reproducible research infrastructure using web ontologies and structured metadata standards, improving efficiency and enabling large-scale collaboration.
