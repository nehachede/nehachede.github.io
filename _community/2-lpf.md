I became a member of the Lila organization in 2015 and received a scholarship to support my undergraduate studies. Over the years, I attended a variety of trainings and seminars aimed at personal growth, skill development, and improving communication and public speaking abilities. 

Through the Peace Ambassador program, I represented the foundation in London, visiting the [Asha Center](https://ashacentre.org/) and learning about international peace-building initiatives. Upon returning, I engaged in programs promoting education, empowerment, and leadership development, including mentoring young women, teaching science and mathematics to girls in grades 8 to 10, and participating in community initiatives such as tree plantation drives and disaster relief efforts, among others.

Through these experiences, I have developed hands-on skills in organizing community initiatives, mentoring, and engaging with diverse groups to create meaningful impact.<br>
